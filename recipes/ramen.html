Veggie Ramen

Description

This is a recipe of some vegetarian ramen for anyone to make. It's a pretty simple process that I borrowed from google. :D

Ingredients

  • 6-8 oz sliced baby portobello mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp sriracha :D

Steps

  1. Measure out all ingredients and prep veggies
  2. Prep and set aside choice of toppings
  3. Mince garlic
